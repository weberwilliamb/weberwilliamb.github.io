Living Tributes!

Friends of North Creek Forest offers a unique opportunity to remember significant life events in a beautiful and everlasting way.

From the birth of a child to a marriage, anniversary, or death of a loved one, a Living Tribute captures the moment and preserves cherished thoughts in a living tree or shrub in North Creek Forest.

“Let’s take our hearts for a walk in the woods and listen to the magic whispers of old trees.”
- Author Unknown
