We look forward to the day when North Creek Forest is widely accessible to the public.

We envision a network of trails that provides opportunities for people to explore a 'piece of the wild' right here in Bothell, and protects habitat for plants and wildlife - promoting a healthy ecosystem for all.

Planning for these trails is managed by the City of Bothell. The Master Planning process will solicit input from the community.
