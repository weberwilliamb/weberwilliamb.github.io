Volunteer Today!

Volunteers are a vital part of keeping North Creek Forest pristine and open to the public. Urban forest's offer so much to a community. Trees absorb and store carbon dioxide (greenhouse gas) for their lifetime. The trees take in carbon dioxide (poisonous to humans) and give off oxygen (crucial for life). We need the communities help in maintaining this precious commodity.

Help us protect the young plants at our restoration sites, table at an outreach event, or help with our education program. There are many ways to get involved both individually and as a group!.

To set up a Group Volunteering event, please email us at: Friends of North Creek Forest.
